Our story

Every piece starts with one ball of yarn

amalola began the way most handmade brands do — with a hook, a skein of yarn and a lot of love. Today every blanket, bootie and little amigurumi friend is still crocheted entirely by hand, one stitch at a time, right here in Palencia.

No two pieces are ever quite the same, and that's exactly the point. When you order from amalola, you're not buying something mass-produced — you're getting something made for your baby.

  • 100% handmade, zero shortcuts
  • Soft, baby-safe yarns and finishes
  • Colors and details you can personalize
