From Local Cocoa Farms to Your Table

Oro Craft Chocolate is handcrafted in Popondetta, in the heart of Oro Province, Papua New Guinea — a region famous for its fertile volcanic soil and generations of cocoa-growing tradition.

Every bar begins with cocoa beans sourced directly from local growers, honoring the land, the farmers, and the flavors that make PNG cocoa some of the most distinctive in the world.

We believe great chocolate should taste like the place it comes from — and support the community that grows it.
