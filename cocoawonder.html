The Story

Chocolate made by a person who wants to know your name.

Jen started Cocoa Wonder around a simple idea: chocolate tastes better when there's a human behind it. No mass production, no shortcuts — just small batches made with intention, shared with neighbors across the Triangle who are looking for something a little sweeter and a lot more personal.
