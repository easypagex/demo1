The artist

A pale young man, a pair of scissors, and a very small town.

Scissor Stories is the work of one artist who sells old and curious books by day and builds paper collages by lamplight — cutting up magazines, book plates, and forgotten photographs into something new.

Every piece is made by hand. No filters, no digital layers — just paper, glue, a very sharp pair of scissors, and whatever strange thought needed cutting out that night.
