About Adi

Adi Dick is a collage artist obsessed with texture, color, and happy accidents. Every piece starts as scraps — old paper, magazines, patterns — and ends up somewhere nobody expected, including Adi.


The rule is simple: don't wait, just create. That means posting new work daily, sharing the process, and turning the mess of collage into finished art, zines, and kits you can hold in your hands.
