The Brudders Way

Small batch, honest ingredients, no compromises.

Brudders started with a simple frustration: most "premium" ice cream is still full of gums, stabilizers, and syrups dressed up in nice packaging. We wanted the real thing — pasture-raised cream, organic milk, and local raw honey doing the work sugar usually does.

Every pint is made in small batches, so what you taste is dairy and honey, not additives. It's the kind of ice cream that used to be normal, before shortcuts became the industry standard.
