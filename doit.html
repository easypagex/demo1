The story

A serious chocolate day job, and no regrets.

It started the way most obsessions do — one good bonbon, then a hundred more. Formal training at École Chocolat turned instinct into technique: proper tempering, balanced ganaches, and a real understanding of where cacao comes from.

Now every batch is documented, tasted, and shared — snapshots of a life where the "day job" happens to be chocolate. That same care goes into every box that leaves the kitchen for a client's table.
