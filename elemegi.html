Meet Nihal — designer, mom, and knitting YouTuber

Every EL EMEĞİ piece begins on Nihal Pamuk's own needles in Nevşehir, in the heart of Cappadocia. As a designer and YouTuber, she shares her techniques with a growing community, and as the mother of two little girls, she designs every pattern to be as gentle on delicate skin as it is charming to look at.

What you see on Instagram is what you get: original designs, small batches, and organic yarns chosen with a mother's care.
