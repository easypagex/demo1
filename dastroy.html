What is this place?

dastroy is the working name of Planas Guillot — an ongoing archive of photographs, doodles, cut-and-paste collages, home-recorded sounds, and whatever else survives from decades of making things by hand.

Nothing here is polished on purpose. It's photographs next to scrawls next to songs, the way a real studio actually looks. If that sounds like your kind of mess, the full archive lives on Instagram.
