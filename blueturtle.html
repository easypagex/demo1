The Maker

Hi, I'm Jackie.

Certified through Ecole Chocolat

Blue Turtle Chocolates started as a question I couldn't stop asking myself: could I actually make chocolate people love, from scratch, with my own hands? Training through Ecole Chocolat gave me the technique — tempering curves, ganache ratios, the chemistry of a good snap.

The rest is still being figured out one batch at a time. I'm not chasing a factory line here. I'm chasing the moment someone bites into a truffle and gets quiet for a second. That's the whole business plan, honestly.

— Jackie 🍫
