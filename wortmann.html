The Artist

A GERMAN COLLAGE ARTIST, WORKING WITH SCISSORS AND SCALPEL

Jens dissects, combines, and layers found and shot photography into new narratives. His collages deconstruct reality and reassemble it — fragmented, condensed, and often unexpectedly reimagined. No digital shortcuts, no undo button: every edge is cut by hand.

His work sits at the crossroads of cubism and surrealism — portraits and bodies rebuilt from torn paper, exploring beauty, form, and the tension between order and chaos.

How A Collage Is Born

FROM PHOTOGRAPH TO PAPER TO PIECE

1.

Source

Photographs are gathered, printed, and sorted — raw material for something new.

2.

Cut

Scalpel and scissors dissect each image into fragments — no digital tools involved.

3.

Layer

Pieces overlap, echo, and collide on the table until a new composition emerges.

4.

Reveal

A finished collage — reality deconstructed, then reassembled into something unexpected.
